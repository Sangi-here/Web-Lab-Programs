Vegetables

Vegetables are edible plant parts, rich in essential vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants that are crucial for human health, supporting everything from proper digestion and immune function to a lower risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and some cancers. Consuming a variety of vegetables provides complex carbohydrates for sustained energy, while their low fat and calorie content aids in weight management. They can be prepared and eaten in many ways, from raw in salads to cooked in soups and stews, making them a versatile and important component of a balanced diet.
